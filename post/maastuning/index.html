A series of tuning methods have been released in modelscope.

Dec 1, 2022 1 min read

Lora, Prefix, Prompt, Adapter, SideTuning are the tuning methods for foundational model tuning and well used in many downstream tasks. We have adopted these methods on basic multi- modal pretrained model and released the code in modelscope lib. The model cards can be accessed in modelscope.

Besides, we also propose and release the new methd U-Tuning in modelscope, you can use it in modelscope.
